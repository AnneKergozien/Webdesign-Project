A meal without wine is called breakfast

Every occasion deserves a glass of wine

Alone or with friends, to have a glass or many, to grab a bite or order every dish of the menu, our wine bar is the perfect place to unwind.

Make it a glass of very good wine

If you are to have only one glass of wine here, we want to make sure it is an amazing one, perfectly fit to your taste. We selected small wineyards and winemakers that are passionate about producing the best wines, focusing on quality, taste and more natural modes of production.
